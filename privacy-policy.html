This Privacy Policy describes how NaN404 Studios ("we," "our," or "us") handles information in connection with our applications and games, including those published on the Meta Horizon Store.

NaN404 Studios is an independent game development studio. Public projects: github.com/ErrorDanOfficial.

2. Information We Collect

NaN404 Studios does not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) directly through our Services. We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses
  • Payment or financial information

Platform-provided data: Meta may provide platform-level identifiers (e.g. Meta Horizon user ID) solely to enable core app functionality. We do not store, log, or share this data.

Technical data: Crash reports may be generated by Meta or Unity Technologies under their own privacy policies. NaN404 Studios does not retain this data.

3. How We Use Information

Any incidentally processed technical data is used solely to improve app stability, debug issues, and understand aggregate usage patterns. We do not use data for advertising or profiling.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or transfer personal information to third parties. Third-party platforms we use:

  • Meta Platforms Technologies: meta.com/legal/privacy-policy

5. Data Deletion

We do not store personal data. If you believe data was inadvertently collected, contact us at nan404studios@proton.me. We respond within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (COPPA). Parents or guardians with concerns may contact us at nan404studios@proton.me.

7. Information Security

Our applications do not contain malicious code, do not request unnecessary permissions, and do not transmit user data to external servers.
